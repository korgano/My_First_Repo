Intel Arc Projected to Release in Q2 2022!

To the disappointment of everyone, Intel's new Arc graphics cards are now expected to release in Q2 2022. Website Moore's Law is Dead released a new set of leaks that covered the projected release date, plus a render of the reference model. The delay is reported to be due to supply chain issues, not drivers.

Everyone who's been waiting for a GPU since the current shortage nightmare has begun now has a new worry. Q2 2022 puts it close to the expected Q3 release for AMD and Nvidia's new cards, making fear of missing out and other buyer's remorse extra potent.
